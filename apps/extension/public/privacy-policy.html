Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-06-02

Summary

Mochi Cast (麻薯投屏) does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All settings and device information are stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local.

Data We Access

  • Page content: To detect video URLs on the current tab only when you open the extension popup.
  • Local network: To discover and control DLNA devices on your Wi-Fi.
  • Storage: To save your preferences and last-used TV device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No browsing history uploaded to any server
  • No third-party data sharing

Permissions

  • <all_urls> — Required to sniff video URLs and send DLNA SOAP requests to local devices.
